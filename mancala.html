The sowing game: Oware

Each player owns the six pits on their near side. On your turn, lift all the seeds from one of your pits and sow them one by one into the pits ahead, going counterclockwise. If your last seed drops into one of your opponent's pits and leaves it holding exactly two or three seeds, you capture them — and sweep backward through any neighbouring pits that also hold two or three. The player who gathers more than half the 48 seeds wins.
